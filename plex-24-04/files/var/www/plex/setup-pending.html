Plex claim required

HTTPS is intentionally gated until you claim this server. This prevents anyone on the public IP from taking over an unclaimed Plex instance.

Option A — SSH tunnel

From your laptop:

ssh -L 8888:127.0.0.1:32400 root@YOUR_DROPLET_IP
# then open http://localhost:8888/web and finish claim

After claiming, enable the public HTTPS proxy:

sudo /opt/enable-plex-proxy.sh

Option B — Claim token

  1. Open https://www.plex.tv/claim and copy the token
  2. On the Droplet run:
sudo /opt/claim-plex.sh claim-XXXXXXXX

This claims the server and enables HTTPS automatically.

After claim, use https://YOUR_DROPLET_IP (or your domain via /opt/setup-plex-domain.sh).
